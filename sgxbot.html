DEVELOPER'S NOTE

With the advancement in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and AI, chatbots are close to being able to converse with humans in a manner which soon may satisfy the Turing test. Today, chatbots serve a multitude of purposes, from providing information and providing automated services, such as incorporating a company. Companies are increasingly subject to stricter compliance rules and regulations and a compliance chatbot could be helpful to business owners and employees. Development took approximately 2 weeks. Powered by BotPress.

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Say "hi" to begin.
  • When presented with choices for an answer, please only select the choice. Do not use the chatbox.
  • Click on the curly arrow on the top right to reset at any time. Say "hi" to begin again.
  • Click on the down arrow on the top right to download the chat.
  • Click on the menu icon on the top right to create new chat sessions.
  • This webapp is only a proof of concept. Broken links and bot malfunction are to be expected.
