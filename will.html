Online Will Generator

DEVELOPER'S NOTE

Legal technology has been making waves within the legal industry, with leading law firms leveraging on powerful AI-driven tools for legal research, document assembly and document review. OCBC made headlines when it lauched its free online will generator for citizens and residents to prepare their wills within 10 minutes on their computers, laptops or mobile devices. While the OCBC online will generator is capable enough in most use cases, my version addresses its lack of customisation and editability once exported. Development took approximately 1 week and is powered by PHPOffice.

With the advancement of legal technology, this could sound the death knell for lawyers and other professionals charging an arm and a leg for simple documents which rarely require substantive legal advice such as wills, Lasting Power of Attorney, trusts, corporate resolutions, non-disclosure agreements, employment agreements and tenancy agreements. A similar document generator for one of these may be developed in future.
